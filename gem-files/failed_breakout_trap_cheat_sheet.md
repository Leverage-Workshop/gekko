**Failed Breakout Trap in a Maturing Uptrend** 

## **Setup Summary:** 

- Uptrend is extended ('long in the tooth'), reaching a prior high/extreme. 

- Price begins to chop in a tight range (distribution). 

- Three failed pushes higher inside the chop, middle push is the biggest. 

- Blue delta builds on top of red delta - aggressive buying absorbed. 

- Final result is a sharp liquidation move downward. 

## **Tactical Reframe:** 

## Phase 1: Overextension 

- Trend has advanced without reinforcement. 

- Price reaches exposed territory and stalls at prior high. 

Phase 2: Stalemate + Feints 

- Chop sets in. Each push is a feint. 

- The second push traps breakout longs. 

- The third push tricks those already offside. 

## Phase 3: Build Without Gain 

- Blue delta clusters stack over red delta. 

- No progress in price = failed offensive. 

## Phase 4: Liquidation Break 

- Sellers take control. 

- Red delta flushes through blue cluster. 

- Trapped buyers exit en masse - price collapses. 

## **Why It Works:** 

- Traps participants who buy into failed momentum. 

- Blue stacked on red = poor long trade location. 

- Red absorbs silently, then reoffers and pushes. 

- Exploits belief that price 'always goes up'. 

**Failed Breakout Trap in a Maturing Uptrend** 

## **How to Spot & Trade It:** 

Visual Cues: 

- Chop zone after extended uptrend. 

- Three pushes up; middle one is biggest. 

- Delta profile shows blue tapering and stacking. 

Tape/DOM: 

- Reoffers on each push. 

- No rebid on final drop. 

Trade Plan: 

- Wait for liquidation candle to break below structure. 

- Entry: retest of breakdown area (former chop support). 

- Target: VWAP or structural low. 

- Stop: just above final failed high.
