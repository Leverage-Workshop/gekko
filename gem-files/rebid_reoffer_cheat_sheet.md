**Rebid / Reoffer Cheat Sheet (Color-Coded - Blue = Buying, Red = Selling)** 

## **Rebid (Blue Reappears After Red Attack):** 

- You're in an up move. 

- Sellers (Red) hit the bid; blue size shrinks. 

- Blue (Buying) refills size at same or higher price. 

- Price either holds or ticks up. 

- Tape shows persistent green prints (buyers taking offers). 

- Interpretation: buyers are holding ground, showing commitment. 

- Tactical Analogy: reinforcements arrive after initial attack and push back. 

## **Reoffer (Red Reappears After Blue Push):** 

- You're in an up move. 

- Buyers (Blue) lift the offer; red size disappears. 

- Red (Selling) reappears at the same or lower level--refills quickly. 

- Tape shows red selling after an attempt to push higher. 

- Price stalls or reverses. 

- Interpretation: sellers are regaining control, rejecting continuation. 

- Tactical Analogy: enemy baited forward, but defenders re-establish position. 

## **DOM Behavior Summary:** 

Rebid (Blue): Red hits bid -> blue reloads -> price holds/grinds up. 

Reoffer (Red): Blue lifts offer -> red reloads -> price stalls or reverses. 

Key signals: 

- Blue reloading fast = buyer conviction. 

- Red stepping in front = aggressive seller control. 

- No follow-through after buying = trap potential. 

- Reoffer after break attempt = rejection of breakout. 

## **Practical Use:** 

- Watch for sequences, not speed. 

- Focus on 1-2 price levels: aggression -> response -> result. 

- Look for signs of structure, reinforcement, and pushback.
